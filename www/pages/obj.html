JOAC
Justice Oputa Annual Conference 2018

The Conference brings together eminent persons, policy makers/public sector representatives, researchers, legal professionals, commerce and industry experts in ongoing conversations and exploration of issues, ideas and analysis of its core areas of concern, which are governance, law and development.

Conference Objectives
  • star_half The inevitability of instituting stronger governance and accountability at public and private sector levels, political party organizations, and the professions.
  • star_half Seek new ways of ensuring strict financial and fiscal discipline especially in the area of public financial management systems as part of the strategy for checking waste and corruption.
  • star_half Working side by side with government and key stakeholders, the Conference will also seek to strengthen efforts to diversify the economy, improve the business environment to attract new investments across sectors that will deliver industrial development for increased economic growth and overall national GDP.
  • star_half The Event ultimately aims to find a pathway to achieving peace, security, and political stability, for sustainable economic advancement and improved human development index.
